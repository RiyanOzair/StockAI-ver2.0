What's Inside

Everything a real
market needs.

From behavioral finance to circuit breakers — StockAI models the complexity of real financial markets.

🤖
LLM-Driven Agents

Each agent is powered by Groq's Llama 3.3. They read market events, form opinions, debate in a forum, and trade autonomously.

📊
Live WebSocket Feed

Price ticks, trade executions, and market events stream live via WebSocket. Every session is visible in real time.

🏦
Order Book Engine

A full limit-order matching engine with bid/ask queues, partial fills, and market orders. Real microstructure.

⚡
Circuit Breakers

Stocks that move more than 10% in a session are automatically halted — just like real exchanges.

🔗
Sector Correlation

Price moves ripple across sectors based on a real correlation matrix. Tech and Finance move together; Energy stands apart.

💬
Agent Forum

Agents post bullish/bearish opinions each day. Watch the sentiment shift as events unfold and positions change.

🏆
Portfolio Race

A live leaderboard races agent portfolios over time. See who compounds the fastest — and who blows up.

🔔
Price Alerts

Set target prices for any stock. Get instant notifications when agents push prices through your thresholds.

🧠
Explainability

Inspect every agent's decision logic, bias distribution, win rate, and maximum drawdown. Full transparency.

How It Works

A full market in
four steps.

01
Configure

Set the number of days, volatility, speed and event intensity. Choose your agent mix.

02
Simulate

Each day runs 3 trading sessions. Agents receive market events and LLM reasoning kicks in.

03
Trade

Orders flow through the matching engine. Prices update, snapshots are saved, charts refresh live.

04
Analyse

Rewind any day's snapshot, compare agent portfolios, inspect the heatmap and financial reports.
